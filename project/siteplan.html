Site Name:
Caring Kitchen: Sourdough

The domain will be CaringKitchen-sourdough.com, which is still available. Caring Kitchen is the name of the small home business run by Carin Gardner. This website focuses on her premium product, 3-Day Sourdough

Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to share all that Carin has learned about making sourdough bread and to make that information available to anyone who is as interested in sourdough as she is.

Scenarios

  • What makes long-fermented sourdough bread different from other bread?
  • Which ingredients make a difference in bread?
  • Is there specific equipment that works best?
  • Is the baker willing to share her recipe and process?

Color Schema

The color palette was created at Coolers.com. The color scheme was originally used for the creator's WDD131 project. This website pertains to the creator's home business as well, so she wanted it to be stylized similarly. Delft Blue and Dun will be the main font colors and backgrounds. The colors chamoise, alloy orange, and russet will be used as accents and borders throughout the site.

Typography

I chose this font (Chilanka) because I wanted a welcoming font with a homespun quality for titles and headings.

I chose this font (Quicksand) because I wanted an open rounded font that paired well with the other font, but was easier to read in paragraphs and information.
